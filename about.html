About Kristopher McCoy

Hi, I’m Kristopher McCoy — a U.S. Army combat veteran and cybersecurity professional with a Master’s degree in Cybersecurity (3.97 GPA) and an ISC2 certification candidate. After years of technical training and hands-on experience, I founded Iron Dillo Cybersecurity to help individuals and small businesses protect themselves in an increasingly complex digital world.
