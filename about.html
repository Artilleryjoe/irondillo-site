A grounded cybersecurity practice built for real life.

Iron Dillo Cybersecurity exists to make cybersecurity more approachable for rural businesses, families, churches, schools, and community groups that often get overlooked or priced out of meaningful support.

Meet Kris McCoy

Kris is a veteran and independent cybersecurity consultant focused on practical readiness. The goal is simple: help people understand what matters most, fix high-impact risks first, and build confidence step by step.

Iron Dillo can provide deeper technical work when needed, including automation and custom tooling, but the core mission starts with trust, usefulness, and plainspoken guidance.
