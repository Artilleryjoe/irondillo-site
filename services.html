Services

Whether you’re running a nonprofit, a family business, or just trying to protect your digital life—I’m here to help you make sense of cybersecurity. Iron Dillo offers no-pressure, veteran-backed services tailored to real people and growing teams.

Cybersecurity Audit (Starter)

A plain-English walkthrough of your current setup. No scare tactics—just what’s exposed, what’s fine, and what needs attention. Perfect for websites, small networks, and cloud-based businesses.

Employee Security Training

Equip your team to spot phishing, social engineering, and weak links. Custom sessions available for remote teams, volunteers, or non-technical staff.

Alert & Incident Translation

Confused by a warning or suspicious email? I’ll break it down, explain the risk (if any), and advise your next move in clear terms.

Light Touch Site Check

I’ll run non-invasive testing of your contact forms, login screens, or public-facing pages to spot basic misconfigurations or leaks.

Add-On: Documentation Help

If you need a privacy policy, terms of use, or a short “security practices” page for clients, I offer lean, readable copy built from your actual practices.
