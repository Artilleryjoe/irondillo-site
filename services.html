Service model

Practical cybersecurity services, from basics to deeper support.

Iron Dillo is an independent cybersecurity practice focused on helping rural and underserved communities take useful next steps without jargon or unnecessary complexity.

Awareness and Training

Practical coaching for phishing, password safety, MFA, and daily habits that reduce risk for teams and households.

Small-Business Security Checkups

Focused reviews to identify high-impact gaps and prioritize sensible next steps for small organizations with limited technical staff.

Backup and Ransomware Readiness

Backup verification, restore planning, and disruption readiness support so recovery is faster and less chaotic.

Website, Email, and Domain Safety Review

Checks for exposed services, risky configurations, and avoidable weak points across core internet-facing systems.

Practical Technical Support

Hands-on help turning findings into action with plain language documentation, implementation guidance, and realistic milestones.

2. Automation and hardening
