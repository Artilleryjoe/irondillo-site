Why Rural Businesses Can’t Ignore Cybersecurity Anymore

When folks think about hackers, they picture big cities and giant companies. Truth is, small rural businesses are just as likely — maybe more likely — to get hit.

Why? Hackers look for easy targets. No firewall, weak passwords, old computers — they love that.

The local diner, the family farm, the feed store — they all run on the same internet as everyone else. One fake email, one stolen password, one old laptop — that’s all it takes.

Good news

  • Use strong, unique passwords.
  • Keep your devices updated.
  • Back up files somewhere offline.
  • Be cautious with strange emails.
  • Get a basic security checkup.

Doing nothing is like leaving your shop door wide open at night.
